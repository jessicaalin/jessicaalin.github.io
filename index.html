About

One of the few Miami natives.
I sing while I do things.
And I dance.

All right. I know what you're thinking: "She's a full-stack developer, UX/UI designer, AND a program manager?! Is she too good to be true?!"

No! I'm real ü¶Ñ Honestly, since I was a kid I always dabbled in the arts and that turned into my design skills. Also as a kid, I would hack my games but didn't realize what I was doing - later in life, I found development and loved the power that comes with being able to build. And I'm naturally organized since I've always enjoyed a good to-do list.

I'm not a genius or anything, but I can work hard and like to research until I figure something it out.

But to give you more details into the tech I use, I'm gonna drop some jargon here:

  • As a full-stack developer, I use HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, jQuery, JavaScript, Node.js, Express, React, and RESTful APIs; currently, I'm learning ARIA and accessibility in web development because that's a huge passion of mine.
  • As a UX/UI designer, I use Sketch, Adobe XD, Figma, InVision, Zeplin, user research, user testing, rapid prototyping, journeys, blueprints, and more! I specialize in service and learning experience design.
  • As a program manager, I use Agile methodologies; I'm a power Trello user.
